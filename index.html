Welcome to the Davis Catalyst Capture the Flag! This project was made entirely by students (in 48 hours no less)!

This is a series of challenges in increasing difficulty. Each level yields a "flag" that allows you to complete it.

Each flag is formatted like "cat{INSERT_FLAG_HERE}". Some challenges may give you the whole flag, some may only give the inside contents. Format at your own discretion.
